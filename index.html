Do Dogs Know Their Name?

Do dogs recognize their name each time that we call them? Yes, they are actually capable of that. Dogs are actually capable of discerning their own name from command words or any string of words. Though this is not something that they will simply learn as they grow up, they will have to be trained by regularly calling them by their name appropriately.

Why Does My Dog Attack Me?

There are several reasons for dogs to show aggression towards their owners.

  • Illness or injury

    Dogs often act aggressive when they feel pain or discomfort in their body. Make sure to take your dog to a veterinarian by any means if you feel like this is the reason before it gets worse for anybody.

  • Fear

    Not every dog has lived a life full of treats and flowers, dogs can also experience traumatic experiences as it grows up. Depending on the background of your dog, make sure to treat them gently and with love. To avoid harm for everyone, make sure to approach dogs with full care.

  • Possessiveness

    Possessiveness can occur with any object. If a dog is holding an object and is not letting anyone else touch it, it is best that you simply let them have it until they get tired of it. If it is an object that you really have to take away, make sure to approach the dog carefully and even use other distractions to get the object.

  • Frustration

    Remember that dogs also value their personal space a lot. Continues violation of their space might cause the dog the feel frustrated and act aggressive.
